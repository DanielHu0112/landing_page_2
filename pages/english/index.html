What we provide

A natural language processing (NLP) software designed for college and graduate students for text analysis.

PLOGIN is currently designed as a "professional academic reader with note-taking capabilities." Utilizing natural language processing (NLP) technology, PLOGIN rapidly structures information to assist students who require extensive reading in filtering data more efficiently, integrating the contents with their own logic.
